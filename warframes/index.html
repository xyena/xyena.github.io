Warframe Guides

Site in progress.

  • What is this site?
    • This site is a compilation of my builds that I frequently use in Warframe that I have developed over the years. Though they were previously displayed in a Google document, I decided I wanted a little more control over the styling of the document, and to gain a little web development experience in the process.
  • Why should I use your guides and not someone else's?
    • These are all builds I have created, not ones that are borrowed from any other source. They have been tested against several missions and are optimized for different kinds of gameplay. Because I play so often and am always experimenting, I can personally guarantee this will be updated as long as I am playing.
